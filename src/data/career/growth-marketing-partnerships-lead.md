#### Role overview

We are hiring a **Growth Marketing & Partnerships Lead** to help shape how TalepNET grows. This role sits across marketing, commercial strategy, and early revenue operations. You will help us clarify positioning, improve demand generation, and build the right partnerships around an early-stage B2B SaaS product.

This is not a pure brand role and it is not a narrow campaign role. We need someone who can think strategically and still execute directly. You should be comfortable moving between messaging, landing pages, paid experiments, outbound support, CRM hygiene, and partnership conversations.

You will work closely with founders, product, and commercial stakeholders. The goal is to build a more disciplined go-to-market motion around a product that solves a real operational problem for procurement teams.

##### What you’ll work on

- Improve how TalepNET is positioned across core ICPs and use cases.
- Build and test growth programs across paid, content, outbound, and partner channels.
- Support early pipeline creation with stronger messaging and campaign structure.
- Help tighten the handoff between marketing activity and commercial follow-up.
- Build a more consistent reporting layer around traffic, lead quality, and pipeline signals.

##### What we’re looking for

- Experience in a **B2B SaaS** environment with visible ownership of growth outcomes.
- Strong commercial judgment, not just channel execution.
- Confidence writing and refining product-facing and market-facing messaging.
- Ability to operate with limited structure and improve it while moving.
- Comfort working closely with founders in an MVP-to-scale transition.

##### Why this role matters

TalepNET is at a stage where go-to-market quality matters more than volume theater. We want someone who can help turn early product direction into a clearer commercial engine and do it with discipline.
