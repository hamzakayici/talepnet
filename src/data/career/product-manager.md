#### Role overview

We are hiring a **Product Manager** to help shape the next stage of TalepNET. This role is about turning product direction into a clearer roadmap, tighter priorities, and better execution across a workflow-heavy B2B SaaS product.

TalepNET sits in a part of software where teams care about structure, visibility, and control. That means product work needs more than feature shipping. It needs good judgment about process design, user roles, operational friction, and where complexity should or should not live.

You will work closely with design, engineering, founders, and go-to-market teams. We are looking for someone who can understand the product deeply, ask better questions, and make the roadmap sharper without overcomplicating it.

##### What you’ll work on

- Drive discovery across procurement workflows, user roles, and operational pain points.
- Translate product direction into clear scopes, priorities, and release decisions.
- Improve how features connect across requests, approvals, purchasing, receiving, and spend visibility.
- Work with engineering and design to keep delivery practical and quality-focused.
- Support product communication across internal teams and customer-facing conversations.

##### What we’re looking for

- Strong product judgment in **B2B SaaS** or workflow-heavy software.
- Experience balancing discovery, prioritization, and delivery in growing product teams.
- Ability to simplify complex operational problems into usable product decisions.
- Comfort working in a direct, low-ceremony environment.
- Clear written and verbal communication across technical and non-technical stakeholders.

##### Why this role matters

TalepNET is moving from a promising foundation toward a more mature product system. This role matters because product quality will depend on better prioritization, clearer workflow thinking, and more disciplined decision-making across the roadmap.
